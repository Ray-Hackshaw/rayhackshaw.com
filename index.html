Ray Hackshaw

Computer Science.
University of Auckland, New Zealand.

I am a computer science student with a passion for web-development and human computer interaction. Through my time at university as well as my own personal research, I have attempted to sculpt an understanding of computer science through the lens of both the logical (tangible) and the human (abstract) perspectives. Through every and any facet of my work, I aim to further explore the significance of this relationship, and hopefully by achieving and maintaining a balance between these two, better the lives of both users and developers around me.
